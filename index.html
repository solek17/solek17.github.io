About me

My name and surname does not matter. I value my privacy at paranoid level and I place it on the top of my priorities. Expose only what is necessary to be exposed!

Who am I? A programmer residing somewhere in central Poland. For a living and for fun I like to combine several lines of code into an interesting whole. Programming goal is not so important as a challenge behind it. I enjoy it and I love it. Despite the passion and experience I consider myself rather as a beginner. I am aware how much I know as well as how much I do not know.

I do not particularly like graphics interfaces and their candy appearance. Don't expect to find any of those in my projects. Good old style terminal applications. This is what I like the most.
